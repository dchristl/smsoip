! With SMSoIP you can send text messages by using your phones Wifi or 3G connection over several providers.

! To make this work do the following steps (if not already done): * Install one or more SMSoIP plugin(s) of your favourite provider(s). SMSoIP needs at least one plugin. * Check out the provider options in the menu to maintain the credentials of the selected provider. * Have a look in the program options for additional/global settings. * Finish. Now you can send text messages.
! For additional usage instructions you can watch video at Youtube or have a look at the Homepage.
! Have fun.
